TalkTalk

Can You Watch This A Sec?

TalkTalk came to us with a direction: Surprisingly Better. The challenge was making that feel native to social without it reading like an ad. I found the answer in a trending format where creators leave their phone recording and walk away. The reaction when they leave is the joke. Using AI and casting, whatever people left behind got surprisingly better while they were gone. Ask someone to watch your cake a second, come back to find it's tripled in size. The brand message was baked into the format. No one had to say a word.

Photoshop IRL

The brief was to show everyone can Photoshop. My thought? It's probably easier than real life. Paint buckets, lassoing, retouching. You could do all of this IRL, but Photoshop just makes it easier. I built an influencer campaign that challenged novices to test their real-world skills against the tools in Photoshop, using Ameziene as the hero creator.
