Play-Doh

Play-Doh Professors

What if kids were the teachers? I flipped the brief entirely. Instead of showing adults how to use Play-Doh, the kids gave the instructions and the adults had to follow. Because "doing it wrong" is your adult brain talking, not theirs. A five-part social series including BTS, guess the creation and dream pet episodes.

AI Design / Social Campaign

TalkTalk

Can You Watch This A Sec?

TalkTalk came to us with a direction: Surprisingly Better. The challenge was making that feel native to social without it reading like an ad. I found the answer in a trending format where creators leave their phone recording and walk away. Using AI and casting, whatever people left behind got surprisingly better while they were gone. Ask someone to watch your cake a second, come back to find it's tripled in size. The brand message was baked into the format. No one had to say a word.
